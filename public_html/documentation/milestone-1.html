!---Purpose, Audience, and Goal of the website--->

Purpose:

The website will be a small business website with focus on the front-end. It will also include a section for
e-commerce (online Yoga classes registration) and a newsletter that sends out a daily mantra.

Audience:

The website is targeted towards private individuals not only interested in taking Yoga classes but with a special interestn in classical Yoga including Kriya rituals and Pranayama techniques for both physical and mental well-being. The philosophy and practices are based on the ancient scriptures of Maharshi Patanali's Yoga Sutras.
