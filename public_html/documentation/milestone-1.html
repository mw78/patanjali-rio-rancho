!---Purpose, Audience, and Goal of the website--->

Purpose:

The website will be a small business website with focus on the front-end. It will also include a form for people
to sign up and receive a daily mantra. In the future it might have a section for some e-commerce
(registration for online Yoga classes). But momentarily this is out of scope.

Audience:

The website is targeted towards private individuals not only interested in taking Yoga classes but with a special interest
in classical Yoga including Kriya rituals and Pranayama techniques for both physical and mental well-being. The philosophy and practices
are based on the ancient scriptures of Maharshi Patanali's Yoga Sutras.

Goal:

The primary goal is to attract individuals with a special interest in classical Yoga and Patanjali so they inquire about classes.
The secondary goal is to use this website for my professional portfolio.
