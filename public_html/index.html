Yoga Chittavritti Nirodha

Maharshi Patanjali, founder of the Yogic Philosophy defines Yog as concentration.

The above saying translates to "stoppage of fluctuations of the mind."

"Yoga is Samadhi - a deep state of concentration."
The stillness of your mind can be attained once you learn how to keep your body still."

"Yoga is Samadhi."
